About the project

Simple, professional lost and found management for campus communities.

Campus Lost & Found helps students and staff report missing items, publish found belongings, and recover property through a clean, responsive interface.

Fast Matching
Clear Reporting
Secure Access
Purpose

Why this platform exists

The problem

Lost and found items are often managed manually, which makes search and recovery slow. People need one place to report items and view matches without confusion.

Our approach

We provide a straightforward interface with structured forms, item matching, and notifications so the process feels reliable and professional.

Features

Designed for everyday use
